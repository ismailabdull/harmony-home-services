Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help me find housing?

Response times vary based on your specific situation and urgency. We prioritize clients at immediate risk of homelessness and typically respond within 24-48 hours for urgent cases. For planning purposes, we can schedule appointments within a week.

Do I need to be referred by a case manager?

No, you can contact us directly. While we work closely with case managers and county agencies, we also accept self-referrals. We'll help you navigate the process and connect you with appropriate resources.

What documents should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring any current identification, proof of income, eviction notices if applicable, and any housing-related documents. Don't worry if you don't have everything - we can help you gather necessary documentation.

Do you provide emergency housing assistance?

Yes, we provide emergency housing assistance for those at immediate risk of homelessness. We can help with emergency rental assistance applications, temporary housing options, and crisis intervention services.

Is there a cost for your services?

Our services are typically covered by county contracts and housing assistance programs. We work with you to determine eligibility and ensure services are accessible regardless of your financial situation.
