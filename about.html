Our Story

Harmony Home Services is a Minnesota-based Housing Stabilization Services (HSS) provider dedicated to helping individuals and families secure and maintain stable housing. We partner with clients, case managers, and county agencies to provide personalized support, advocacy, and resources that empower our clients to overcome barriers to housing.

We specialize in serving individuals who are at risk of homelessness, transitioning from institutions or shelters, or facing challenges due to disabilities, financial hardship, or complex life circumstances. Our team offers compassionate, professional assistance to help clients navigate housing programs, apply for benefits, and find safe, affordable, and sustainable living arrangements.

Founded with a vision of creating pathways to housing stability, we have been serving the Minnesota community with dedication and expertise. Our approach is rooted in understanding that every client's journey is unique, and we tailor our services to meet individual needs and circumstances.
