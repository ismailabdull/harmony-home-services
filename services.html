Our Services

Comprehensive Housing Stabilization Services designed to help you secure and maintain stable housing in Minnesota.

Housing Advocacy

Our professional advocacy services help you navigate complex housing systems and understand your rights as a tenant or housing applicant.

  • Tenant rights education and support
  • Landlord-tenant dispute resolution
  • Housing discrimination assistance
  • Legal resource referrals
  • Rights-based housing counseling

Housing Search & Placement

We help you find safe, affordable, and accessible housing options that meet your specific needs and circumstances.

  • Comprehensive housing search assistance
  • Affordability analysis and budgeting
  • Accessibility assessment for disabilities
  • Neighborhood and community research
  • Application assistance and follow-up

Benefits Application Support

Navigating housing assistance programs can be overwhelming. We provide expert help with applications and documentation.

  • Section 8 and public housing applications
  • Emergency rental assistance programs
  • Disability benefits coordination
  • Documentation gathering and organization
  • Appeal assistance if needed

Case Management

Ongoing support and coordination with case managers, county agencies, and other service providers to ensure comprehensive care.

  • Regular check-ins and progress monitoring
  • Coordination with multiple service providers
  • Goal setting and achievement tracking
  • Crisis intervention and support
  • Resource connection and referrals

Housing Stability Support

Maintaining housing once secured is crucial. We provide ongoing support to help you stay housed and build stability.

  • Rent payment assistance coordination

  • Financial literacy and budgeting
  • Community resource connections
